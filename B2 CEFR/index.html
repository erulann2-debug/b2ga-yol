Eng muhim bo'lim

Gap qurish — bosqichma-bosqich

Sen grammatikani bilasan. Endi uni ishlatishni o'rganish vaqti. Har bir mashqni yozib, keyin ovoz chiqarib ayt.

1

Present Simple — oddiy gap

S + V(s/es) + ...  |  S + don't/doesn't + V + ...
  • I work in a school.
  • She doesn't like coffee.
  • They usually watch TV at night.

Sen yoz: o'zing haqingda 3 ta gap — ish, sevimli narsa, odating.

Boshlang: "I usually…" — keyin davom ettirish oson bo'ladi.

2

Past Simple — kecha nima qilding?

S + V(ed / irregular) + ...  |  S + didn't + V + ...
  • I watched a film yesterday.
  • She didn't come to class.
  • We went to a cafe.

Kechagi kuning haqida 3 ta gap qur. Ertalabdan kechgacha nima qilding?

Boshlang: "Yesterday I woke up at…"

3

Going to / Will — reja

S + am/is/are + going to + V  |  S + will + V
  • I'm going to study English tonight.
  • She will call you tomorrow.
  • We're going to visit Samarkand next week.

Keyingi hafta rejalaringdan 3 ta gap qur.

4

Gap uzaytirish — connector

... because ...  |  ... but ...  |  ... so ...  |  ... and ...
  • I like English because it's useful for work.
  • I wanted to go out but it was raining.
  • I was tired so I went to bed early.

Oldingi gapinglardan birini kengaytir — because, but, so qo'sh.

Bu eng muhim qadam. Qisqa gap emas, uzun gap qurishni o'rganish — B1 va B2 ning asosi.

★

Kunlik mini-mashq — 10 daqiqa

  • Kecha haqida 2 ta gap yoz (Past Simple)
  • Bugun haqida 2 ta gap yoz (Present Simple)
  • Ertaga haqida 2 ta gap yoz (Going to)
  • Bitta gapga because yoki but qo'sh
  • Ovoz yozib ayt — yozish yetarli emas

A2 — Hozir

Qo'rquvni yo'q qilish uchun eng oddiy mavzular.

B1

Kengaytirilgan nutq

Uzunroq, mantiqli gap qurishlar.

  • Past: I used to, I've been to…
  • Problem: The problem is, We could…
  • Story: Suddenly, After a while…
  • Compare: A is better than B…
B2 — Maqsad

Natural speaking

Erkin, tabiiy, ishonchli nutq.

  • Debate: I see your point, but…
  • Complex: From what I can tell…
  • Abstract: texnologiya, jamiyat
  • Defend: My point is that…

Grammatika

A2 → B2 grammatika mavzulari

Siz A2 darajadasiz va asosiy zamonlarni bilasiz. Quyidagi tartibda o'qing.

1

Connectors

because, but, so, and, although, however

2

Present Perfect

I have done, She has been, Have you ever…?

3

Conditional 1

If I study, I will pass. If it rains, we'll stay.

4

Conditional 2

If I had money, I would travel. Xayoliy vaziyat.

5

Passive voice

It was built in 1900. English is spoken worldwide.

6

Reported speech

She said (that) she was tired. He told me to wait.

7

Relative clauses

The book which I read… The man who called…

8

Gerund vs Infinitive

I enjoy reading. I want to learn. I stopped smoking.

9

Articles

a/an/the — qachon ishlatish va qachon tashlab ketish.

10

Word formation

un-, re-, dis-, -ness, -ful, -er (prefiks va suffiks)

O'rganish usullari

So'z va ko'nikma texnikalari

Nuriya Akhmedovnaning materiallaridan olingan eng samarali usullar.

S

Spaced Repetition

Yangi so'zni 10 min, 50 min, ertasi kuni, 2 kundan keyin takrorlash. Kamida 7 marta duch kelish kerak.

A

Associative Learning

So'zni tarjima bilan emas, aniq situatsiya bilan yodlash. Word → Definition → Specific Situation.

W

Word Root texnikasi

So'z ildizini bilib, 10–20 yangi so'zni bir vaqtda tushunish. bio = hayot → biology, biography.

H

Habit Stacking

Yangi ingliz odatini eski odatga bog'lash. Choy → 2 min so'z. Telefon quvvat → 1 min speaking.

P

Pomodoro

25 min ish + 5 min dam. 3 blok: so'zlar / listening+reading / grammar+writing. Telefon o'chirilgan holda.

C

Learning in Chunks

So'zlarni alohida emas, collocation sifatida yodlash. "Strong rain", "make a decision", "take a break".

B2 Frazalar

Speaking uchun B2 frazalar

Bu frazalarni ishlatganda nutqingiz B2 darajasiga chiqadi.

Fikr bildirish

From what I can tell…Ko'rib/tushunishimcha…
The way I see it…Menimcha, men ko'rganim bo'yicha…
If I'm being honest…Agar rostini aytsam…
At the end of the day…Oxir-oqibat…
I have mixed feelings about it.Ikki xil his-tuyg'udaman.

Bahslashuv

I see your point, but…Fikringizni tushundim, lekin…
That's not necessarily true…Bu albatta to'g'ri emas…
I wouldn't say that…Men buni aytmagan bo'lardim…
Let's not jump to conclusions.Shoshilinch xulosa qilmaylik.
There's no doubt that…Shubhasiz…

Murakkab struktura

The main reason is that…Asosiy sabab shundaki…
What I mean is…Demoqchi bo'lganim…
It's more complicated than that.U bundan ham murakkabroq.
For the most part…Asosan / ko'p jihatdan…
I'm trying to make sense of it.Buni tushunishga urinyapman.
